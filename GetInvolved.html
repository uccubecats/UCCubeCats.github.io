Get Involved

How to Join

Join our organisation and become part of some cool space projects!

CubeCats is always looking for UC students who have an interest in software development, electronics, design, analysis, and systems engineering for space missions. Although we are a predominantly an undergraduate organization and located within the Department of Aerospace Engineering and Engineering Mechanics, we accept university students of every level (graduate and undergraduate) and any major who want to be involved. The nature of most of our projects makes interdisiplinary work essential, including potential opportunities for non-engineering students.

There are several options if you are interested in joining:

  • Come to our introductory meeting held at the beginning of each academic year (typically late August/early September), in which we will outline our work, explain how to get involved, and chat with prospective members.
  • Come to one of our public meetings (see our Calendar for information) and talk with us directly.
  • Contact us directly, and tell us about your interest in joining CubeCats, any relevant past experience or skills, and what you might be interested in working on for CubeCats.
